The Process

From Raw Data to Confident Decision

Six layers of intelligence between a scraped listing and your fair value score.

Step 01
Daily Data Scraping
Our backend scrapes all major South African car listing platforms daily, collecting every new and updated listing in real time.
vehicle_idtitlewebsite_urlcreated_at
Step 02
Normalisation & Deduplication
Raw data is normalised across different platform formats. Duplicate listings are identified and merged using vehicle_id matching.
makemodelvariantyearmileagefuel_typetransmission
Step 03
Seller Trust Scoring
Each seller is assigned a trust score based on their rating, number of reviews, NADA membership status, VIN requirement, and featured status.
seller_ratingseller_rating_countagent_nada_memberagent_vin_required
Step 04
Provincial Price Indexing
Using geo-coordinates and province data, we build a provincial price index to compare like-for-like prices across all 9 provinces.
provinceagent_localityagent_coords_latagent_coords_lng
Step 05
ML Fair Value Calculation
Our machine learning model calculates a fair value estimate for every listing, adjusting for mileage, condition, province, fuel type, and Rand fluctuations.
pricemonthly_priceconditionfinance_availablecolour
Step 06
You Buy With Confidence
Search, filter, compare, and check the fair value score on any listing. Walk into any negotiation with data - and never overpay again.

Trim / spec level - significant price differentiator
price
Listed asking price in ZAR - primary valuation input
monthly_price

Odometer reading - key depreciation variable

Petrol/Diesel/Hybrid/EV - premium/discount factor
transmission

Frequently Asked Questions

Our backend scrapes all major South African vehicle listing platforms daily, including AutoTrader ZA, Cars.co.za, and the Motus Select platforms. Each listing is normalised, deduplicated, and stored with a full set of data fields covering vehicle specs, pricing, seller information, and geolocation.
The platform runs a full data refresh daily. New listings are added, price changes are tracked via the updated_at field, and sold or removed listings are flagged.
The Fair Value Score is an Machine Learning model applied on whether a vehicle's listed price is below, at, or above the market average for its specific make, model, year, variant, mileage, province, fuel type, and condition. Backtesting against historical sold prices shows approximately 94% accuracy within a 10% price band. It is indicative, not a formal valuation.
