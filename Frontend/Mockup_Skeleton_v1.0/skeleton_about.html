FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

[▼] How accurate are your price estimates?

Our ML model analyzes 70,000+ active listings and achieves accuracy within ±R 15,000 (approximately 6-8%) for most vehicles. Accuracy is highest for popular makes and models with sufficient data.

[▼] Is AutoInsight ZA free to use?

Yes! All features including search, price estimates, and market insights are completely free for consumers. We may introduce premium features in the future.

[▼] How often is your database updated?

We refresh our database daily at 2:00 AM. New listings typically appear within 24 hours of being posted on source platforms.

[▼] Do you sell cars directly?

No. We're a data and analytics platform. We help you make informed decisions but you purchase directly from dealers or private sellers via the original listing platforms.

[▼] Which provinces do you cover?

We cover all 9 South African provinces: Gauteng, Western Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, Free State, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, North West, and Northern Cape.

[▼] Can I save my searches or favorite vehicles?

This feature is coming soon! For now, you can bookmark specific search URLs in your browser.
