📈 Price Trends

Average used car prices decreased by 1.8% in February, marking the third consecutive month of decline. SUVs and bakkies showing strongest demand.

Gauteng remains the largest market (42% of listings). Western Cape shows 7% higher average prices due to luxury vehicle concentration.
